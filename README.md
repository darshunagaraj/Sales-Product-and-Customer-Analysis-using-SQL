# Sales-Product-and-Customer-Analysis-using-SQL
This project analyses sales, product and customer data using SQL to generate meaningful business insights. The analysis focuses on sales performance, customer behaviour and product trends to support data-driven decision making.

## QUESTIONS (KPIs)
--Calculate the total sales amount for each customer.
--Calculate the total sales amount for each product.
--Find customers who have placed more than 5 orders.
--Calculate the average sales amount per order.
--Find total sales grouped by product category.
--Show all orders where the shipping date is later than the due date.
--Calculate the total quantity sold for each product category.
--Find the top 5 customers based on total sales amount.
--Show monthly sales trends (year and month wise).
--Display customers who were created after 1st January 2020.
--Calculate the average product cost for each category.
--Show all orders where the quantity sold is greater than 10.
--Find products that have never been sold.
--Calculate total sales amount by customer country.
--Count the number of customers by gender.
